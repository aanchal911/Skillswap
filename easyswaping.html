Easy Skill Swapping

1. Add Your Skills

Start by sharing what you’re good at. Add detailed skill descriptions to make your profile stand out.

2. Explore the Community

Search for skills you want to learn. Use filters and browse by categories or time preferences.

3. Propose a Swap

Found someone who matches your interest? Send them a swap request with your availability and proposal.

4. Accept and Start Learning

Once both parties agree, you’re all set! Start your knowledge exchange with mutual respect and fun.
